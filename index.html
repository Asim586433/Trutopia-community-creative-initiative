About Trutopia

Our Mission

To nurture a caring community where everyone’s basic needs are met and every voice can imagine a better world.

Our Vision

A society where mutual aid and art intersect, breaking down barriers and building connection.

Our Story

Trutopia started in 2018 with a single free pantry. Today we run pop‑up shops, workshops, and the Trutopia Project.

Community Values

Dignity, inclusion, creativity, collective care. Everyone has something to give.
